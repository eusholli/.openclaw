# Nokia Intelligence Report

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Company Focus (Current Intelligence)

Nokia is positioning itself as the architect of **AI-Native Networks**, framing the network as a "distributed nervous system" for real-time intelligence. They are emphasizing open, AI-ready, and cloud-ready infrastructure as the bridge from 5G-Advanced to 6G.

### MWC 2026 Themes (March 2-5, Barcelona)
- **Advancing Connectivity for the AI Era:** Nokia's central theme, focusing on AI-native networks and profitable AI infrastructure.
- **AI-Native & AI-RAN:** Showcasing GPU-accelerated AI-RAN for spectral efficiency and beamforming. Key demo includes **Agentic AI-powered network slicing** in collaboration with **AWS, du, and Orange** (world-first).
- **Immersive Voice and Audio (IVAS):** Public demo of 3GPP IVAS for spatial calling and head-tracking in XR/VR.
- **6G & The New Power Stack:** Discussions on the integration of 6G, Cloud, Edge, and AI. Demos of 6G prototypes and XR Audio experiences.
- **Open RAN Evolution:** Ari Kynäslahti (CTO) speaking at the O-RAN Alliance Summit on operator experiences and the path to 6G openness.

### Recent Activity (2026)
- **Feb 26, 2026:** Deployed AI-ready networks in **Telefónica's** Spain Edge data centers.
- **Feb 24, 2026:** Announced agentic AI network slicing with AWS, du, and Orange.
- **Feb 23, 2026:** Demonstrated quantum-safe optical transport with **KDDI** for AI data centers.
- **Feb 2026:** Partnered with Fraunhofer IIS for spatial voice calling (IVAS) demo.
