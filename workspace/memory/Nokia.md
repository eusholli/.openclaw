# Nokia Intelligence Report

**Last Updated:** February 27, 2026

## Company Focus (Current Intelligence)

Nokia is positioning itself as a leader in connectivity for the **AI Supercycle**, focusing on high-performance secure networks, autonomous operations, and programmable monetization. 

### MWC 2026 Themes (March 2-5, Barcelona)
- **Advancing Connectivity for the AI Era:** Nokia's central theme for MWC 2026, focusing on "AI-native" networks and infrastructure.
- **Immersive Voice and Audio (IVAS):** First public demo of 3GPP IVAS interoperability with spatial calling and head-tracking.
- **Agentic AI Network Slicing:** Building on their Feb 2026 collaboration with AWS, Nokia is demoing autonomous, intent-based 5G-Advanced network slicing managed by AI agents.
- **6G Foundations:** Demos of 6G XR Audio and discussions on the "New Power Stack" (6G, Cloud, Edge, AI).

### Strategic Focus (2025-2026)
- **AI Infrastructure:** Following a late 2025 reorganization, Nokia has doubled down on AI-ready infrastructure, including quantum-safe optical transport (KDDI demo) and AIOps for data centers.
- **Partner Ecosystem:** Deep integration with cloud hyperscalers (AWS, Google Cloud, Microsoft) and chipmakers (AMD, Intel, Qualcomm, NVIDIA) to co-create AI-ready networks.
