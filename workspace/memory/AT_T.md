# AT&T

## Profile
- **Role:** Leading US telecommunications provider, aggressively transitioning to Open RAN and fiber-centric growth.
- **Key Strategy:** "Fiber-first" expansion and massive Open RAN deployment with Ericsson.

## Recent Activity (Feb 2026)
- **MWC 2026 Collaboration (Feb 19):** Partnered with Ericsson and Aira Technologies to demonstrate GenAI-driven "Intent-to-rApp" pipelines, converting operator intent into deployable RAN apps on Ericsson's EIAP.
- **Lumen Fiber Acquisition (Feb 2026):** Finalized acquisition of Lumen's Mass Markets fiber business, adding 1M+ subscribers across 32 states.
- **NYC Pension Settlement (Feb 25):** Settled lawsuit regarding workforce demographic disclosures for the 2026 annual meeting.
- **Network API Alliance (Feb 2026):** Founding member of the Alliance for Critical Infrastructure and partner in Aduna (unified US network API with T-Mobile/Verizon).
