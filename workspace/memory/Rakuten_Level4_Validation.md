# Rakuten Level 4 RAN Energy Efficiency Validation

## Latest
- **Feb 19, 2026**: World's first TM Forum Level 4 Autonomy Validation for RAN Energy Efficiency Optimization in a live Open RAN network.
- Resulted in ~20% RAN energy conservation.
- Powered by Rakuten Symphony RIC platform and machine learning rApps.

## Profile
- **Standard**: TM Forum GB1059H (Autonomous Networks Level 4).
- **Technology**: Intent-driven, closed-loop automation with minimal human intervention.
- **Components**: Rakuten Symphony platform, in-house RIC, proprietary rApps.
