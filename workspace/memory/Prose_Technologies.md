# Prose Technologies Intelligence Report

**Last Updated:** February 26, 2026

## Company Focus (Current Intelligence)

**Prose Technologies (PROSE)** is a leading global provider of advanced wireless communication solutions, specializing in base station antennas, microwave antennas, and coverage systems. Spun off from the **Rosenberger Group** in 2022, PROSE combines German engineering heritage with a global manufacturing footprint.

### Key Differentiators
- **Market Leadership**: Ranked #2 globally by ABI Research in the passive base station antenna market (approx. 9.2% market share).
- **Open RAN Commitment**: Heavily invested in the Open RAN ecosystem, providing O-RAN radios and sub-systems designed for multi-vendor interoperability.
- **Innovation Focus**: Known for "Green Manufacturing" and low wind-load antenna designs (PROTREEM series) that reduce site structural requirements and energy consumption.
- **Global Footprint**: Over 3,500 employees across 40+ subsidiaries, with major R&D and manufacturing centers in Germany, China, India, and the US.

### Recent Activity (2025-2026)
- **MWC 2026**: Showcasing Level 4 Autonomous Network integrations and AI-RAN solutions.
- **5.5G Projects**: Partnered on major infrastructure projects, including 5.5G coverage for the Burj Khalifa.
- **India Expansion**: Significant focus on the Indian market, positioning it as a cornerstone for global supply chain and R&D operations.
