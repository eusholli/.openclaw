## 💡 Recommended Discussion Points & Speaking Points

### 1. AT&T's Long-Term RAN Vision (Strategic)
- **Ask:** Where does AT&T see its RAN vendor mix evolving post-Ericsson contract? Is there appetite for a multi-vendor strategy?
- **Speaking Point:** Rakuten Symphony's field-proven track record in Japan (60K+ sites, 9M subscribers) demonstrates scalability and operational excellence that can de-risk AT&T's expansion.
- **Context:** AT&T is targeting 70% traffic on Open RAN by late 2026; they need proven partners for scale.

### 2. OSS Transformation & Network Automation
- **Ask:** What are AT&T's key pain points in OSS? Where do they see the biggest opportunity for automation?
- **Speaking Point:** Rakuten Symphony's AI-driven OSS capabilities (Knowledge Hub, automation) can accelerate AT&T's "Intent-to-rApp" vision.
- **Context:** AT&T's MWC 2026 demo with Aira shows focus on GenAI for RAN automation—Symphony's AI expertise aligns.

### 3. Lab Entry Commitment (Tactical)
- **Ask:** What is the timeline for lab evaluation? What criteria will define success?
- **Speaking Point:** Propose a concrete timeline: Lab entry Q3 2026, with evaluation wrapping up by year-end.
- **Context:** AT&T is in the middle of Ericsson rollout; positioning Symphony as a "backup" or "expansion" partner for resilience makes strategic sense.

### 4. RMI / Rakuten Mobile Updates
- **Share:** Rakuten Symphony achieved first full-year operating profit (FY2025).
- **Share:** All three O-RAN Alliance certifications secured (Jan 2026).
- **Share:** 1&1 Germany deployment progressing well—can serve as European reference.

### 5. Collaboration Areas: RAN + OSS
- **Propose:** Joint proof-of-concept for OSS automation or RAN optimization.
- **Position:** Symphony can complement Ericsson's RAN with best-in-class OSS/automation, creating a "best-of-breed" approach.
- **Leverage:** Existing AT&T partnership (site management) as a foundation for deeper collaboration.

2. **Vendor Lock-in:** AT&T is moving to Open RAN; emphasize multi-vendor interoperability.
3. **US Market Concerns:** Be ready to address any regulatory or supply chain concerns.
