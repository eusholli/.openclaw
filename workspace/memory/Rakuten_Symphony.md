# Rakuten Symphony Intelligence Report

**Last Updated:** February 18, 2026

## Company Focus (Current Intelligence)

Rakuten Symphony is a telecom technology company and division of Rakuten Group, focused on delivering cloud-native, AI-powered Open RAN solutions. Their core expertise is **Open RAN (O-RAN)** — an open, interoperable radio access network architecture that breaks vendor lock-in through standardized interfaces, cloud-native software, and multi-vendor hardware.

### Key Differentiators
- **Field-proven at scale**: Powers Rakuten Mobile's network in Japan — the world's first fully open, cloud-native nationwide 5G/4G network
  - 9 million subscribers
  - 60,000+ sites (macro, small cells, femto)
  - 99.9% population coverage
  - Top-ranked 5G speeds in Japan (Opensignal)

### Major Deployments
- **Japan (Rakuten Mobile)**: Largest Open RAN deployment globally (300K+ cells)
- **Germany (1&1)**: Europe's first fully virtualized nationwide 5G Open RAN
- **Ukraine**: Infrastructure rebuild
- **US (AT&T)**: Extended partnership for site management to accelerate Open RAN rollout
