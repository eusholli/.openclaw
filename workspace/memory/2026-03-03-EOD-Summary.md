# MWC 2026 Day 2 EOD Summary — March 3, 2026

Day 2 in Barcelona solidified "Agentic AI" and "AI-Native 6G" as the dominant strategic pillars for 2026-2030.

### 🚀 Key Announcements & Milestones
*   **AI-Native RAN Call:** **AT&T**, **Ericsson**, and **Intel** achieved the first AI-Native RAN call on a Xeon 6 SoC, moving the $14B Open RAN transition into functional reality.
*   **Rakuten x Samsung:** **Rakuten Mobile** selected **Samsung** as a nationwide Open RAN radio provider, while successfully validating Samsung's vRAN/vCore on the Rakuten Cloud.
*   **Nokia AI-RAN Roadmap:** Nokia confirmed its first commercial **AI-RAN** products for 2027, with field trials starting in late 2026.
*   **6G Coalition:** **NVIDIA** launched a global 6G coalition with **Nokia, Ericsson, BT, DT, SKT, SoftBank, and T-Mobile** to build AI-native software-defined networks.
*   **Satellite Mobile:** **Starlink** and **Deutsche Telekom** announced a Europe-based satellite mobile service for 2028.

### 📅 Meeting Momentum: AT&T x Rakuten Symphony (Mar 4)
*   **Joint Presence:** Rob Soni (VP RAN, AT&T) appeared at the Rakuten booth for a session on "Building the Intelligent RAN," signaling strong technical and strategic alignment ahead of tomorrow's meeting.
*   **Validation:** Samsung's deal with Rakuten and the successful AT&T AI-RAN call provide a "field-proven" backdrop for tomorrow's discussions on intelligent network design.

### 🧠 Strategic Themes
*   **Agentic AI:** Shifting from basic automation to autonomous agents (Samsung's "agentic companion" and Ericsson's "Agentic rApps").
*   **AI-as-a-Service:** Nokia and others exploring new monetization models for enterprise AI.
*   **6G Native AI:** Consensus that 6G must be AI-native from inception (Qualcomm X105 modem and Intel Xeon 6+).
