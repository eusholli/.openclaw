## Full Fibre (FTTP) Rollout

### Investment
- **£15 billion** programme
- Target: **25 million premises** by end of 2026
- Extended goal: **30 million by 2030**

### Current Status (Feb 2026) - Tavily Verified
- **Premises passed:** 21.4 million (early 2026)
- **Premises connected:** 8.2 million (~38% take-up)
- **Rural coverage:** 5.9 million premises
- **Build pace:** Over 1 million premises added per quarter (8th consecutive quarter)
- **Take-up rate:** ~38%

### 2026 Targets
- FY26 (Mar 2026): Up to **5 million new premises** passed
- 25M goal: Expected Oct-Nov 2026
- Additional £5B planned to extend beyond 25M

## PSTN Switch-Off

- **Deadline:** January 31, 2027
- **Current status:** ~2.8 million lines still on PSTN
- **Business lines at risk:** 500K+ business lines haven't migrated
- **Price hikes:** Legacy line prices double by October 2026
- **Additional concerns:** 12K+ lift lines, ~500 CCTV lines need upgrading
- **Quote (James Lilley, Director of All-IP):** "There's no time left to stall... The PSTN analogue network is obsolete, becoming harder to maintain and significantly more expensive to run."

### Key Dates
- Oct 2026: Copper line prices double
- Jan 31, 2027: PSTN switched off (locked deadline, no further delays planned)
