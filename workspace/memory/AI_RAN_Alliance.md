# AI-RAN Alliance

## Profile
- **Role**: Global industry alliance focusing on integrating Artificial Intelligence (AI) with Radio Access Network (RAN) technology to improve network efficiency, reduce power consumption, and unlock new revenue opportunities.
- **Founding Members**: NVIDIA, Nokia, Ericsson, SoftBank, Arm, AWS, Microsoft, Samsung, T-Mobile, and others.
- **Scale**: Over 130 member companies as of March 2026.
