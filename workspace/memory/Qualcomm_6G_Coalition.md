# Qualcomm 6G Coalition

## Profile
- **Role**: A strategic global initiative led by Qualcomm to accelerate the development and deployment of **AI-native 6G** mobile networks.
- **Key Members**: Includes over 40 global industry leaders such as **Samsung, Google, Microsoft, Amazon, T-Mobile, Reliance Jio, Nokia, and Ericsson**.

## Latest
- **Mar 4, 2026**: Focus areas include AI-native connectivity, wide-area sensing, high-performance computing, and spectrum efficiency (centimeter-wave technology). Qualcomm predicts that **AI will constitute 30% of all cellular traffic by 2034**, positioning 6G as essential for the "AI revolution".
- **Mar 4, 2026**: Unveiled the **X105 5G Modem with integrated AI** for real-time link adaptation and power optimization.
- **Mar 4, 2026 (MWC Barcelona)**: Formally launched at MWC with a clear roadmap targeting **commercial 6G systems starting from 2029**.
- **Mar 2, 2026**: Commitment to 6G trajectory towards commercialization; introduction of the Qualcomm X105 5G Modem-RF (R19-Ready).
