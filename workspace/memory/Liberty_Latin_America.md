# Liberty Latin America (LLA) - Latest Intelligence

**Last Updated:** Feb 27, 2026

## Company Focus (Current Intelligence)

Liberty Latin America (LLA) is currently executing a strategy centered on **organic growth, value unlocking, and infrastructure-led B2B expansion**. Following their FY2025 results (reported Feb 18, 2026), the company is focused on the following pillars for 2026:

*   **Cloud & AI Transformation:** On Feb 17, 2026, LLA announced a major 5-year strategic partnership with **AWS** to modernize its IT and ICT offerings. This includes migrating 500+ workloads, deploying AWS Outposts for edge computing in Panama and Colombia, and leveraging AI for internal efficiency (e.g., call centers) and B2B services.
*   **Infrastructure & Subsea (MANTA):** LLA is progressing with the **MANTA subsea cable system** (partnering with Gold Data and Sparkle). This ~5,600 km system connecting the US, Mexico, Panama, and Colombia is a key component of their 2026-2027 infrastructure capex, aiming for high-capacity, low-latency B2B connectivity.
*   **Operational Recovery:** A primary focus for 2026 is the rebuild and recovery of operations in **Jamaica** following the catastrophic impact of Hurricane Melissa (Category 5) in Q4 2025. Mobile services are restored, but fixed network rebuilds continue through 2026.
*   **Financial Discipline:** LLA is targeting margin expansion through cost reductions and expects a second-half-weighted performance in 2026 due to the timing of insurance proceeds and Jamaica recovery.
