# Verizon

## Company Overview
- **Type:** Telecom operator (NYSE: VZ)
- **Headquarters:** New York
- **CEO:** Dan Schulman (appointed Oct 2025, former PayPal CEO)
- **CFO:** Tony Skiadas (since May 2023)
- **Former CEO:** Hans Vestberg (2018-2025) → Special Advisor

## Key Executives

### Dan Schulman - CEO (Oct 2025-present)
- **Background:** Former President & CEO of PayPal (2014-2023), grew revenue from $8B to $30B
- **Prior:** AT&T (18+ years, President), Priceline (CEO), Virgin Mobile USA (Founding CEO), American Express (Group President)
- **Education:** BA Economics (Middlebury), MBA Finance (NYU Stern)
- **Focus:** Innovation, AI, customer experience, cost transformation

### Hans Vestberg - Former CEO (2018-2025)
- **Background:** 25+ years at Ericsson (CEO 2010-2016), joined Verizon 2017 as CTO/President Global Networks
- **Tenure:** Pioneered 5G Ultra Wideband, nationwide 5G launch (2022)
- **Current:** Special Advisor through Oct 2026, Board member until 2026 meeting
- **Other Boards:** BlackRock, UN Foundation, Broadband Commission

### Tony Skiadas - CFO (May 2023-present)
- **Career:** Joined Verizon/Bell Atlantic in 1996, held accounting/finance roles including Controller and SVP Finance Operations
- **Education:** MBA (Seton Hall), BS Accounting (University of Delaware), CPA
